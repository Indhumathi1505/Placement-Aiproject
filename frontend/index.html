About the System
Intelligence Meets Career
Placement Strategy

PlaceIQ is a comprehensive AI-powered platform designed to bridge the gap between students and their career goals. By analyzing your academic profile, skills, projects, and experience, our system generates a personalized placement intelligence report.

The platform uses state-of-the-art language models from Hugging Face — including sentence-transformers and Mistral-7B — to understand your profile in context, compare it against real company requirements, and give you an honest, data-driven assessment.

Unlike generic career tools, PlaceIQ learns from thousands of placement data points to give you company-specific predictions with actionable improvement paths.
